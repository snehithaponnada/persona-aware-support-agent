# Subscription Management Guide

## Upgrade Subscription

1. Navigate to the Billing section.
2. Select Upgrade Plan.
3. Choose the desired subscription tier.
4. Confirm payment details.
5. Submit the upgrade request.

---

## Downgrade Subscription

1. Open Account Settings.
2. Select Subscription Management.
3. Choose Downgrade Plan.
4. Confirm changes.

---

## Cancel Subscription

1. Navigate to Billing Settings.
2. Select Cancel Subscription.
3. Complete the cancellation process.

Cancellation becomes effective at the end of the current billing cycle.

---

## Reactivate Subscription

Customers may reactivate subscriptions at any time before account deletion.
