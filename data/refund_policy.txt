Refund Policy

Eligible Refund Cases:

* Duplicate payments
* Incorrect charges
* Service disruptions caused by platform errors

Required Information:

* Transaction ID
* Registered email address
* Description of the issue

Processing Timeline:

* Refund requests are reviewed within 5 business days.
* Approved refunds are processed within 7 business days.

Non-Refundable Cases:

* Partial subscription usage
* User-caused configuration errors
* Requests submitted after the refund eligibility period

Escalation:

Complex refund requests may require manual review by the billing department.
