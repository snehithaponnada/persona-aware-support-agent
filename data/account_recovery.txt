Account Recovery Guide

If a customer loses access to their account:

1. Click the "Forgot Password" option on the login page.
2. Enter the registered email address.
3. Check the inbox for the password reset link.
4. Create a new password that meets security requirements.
5. Log in using the new password.

Common Issues:

* Reset email not received:

  * Check spam or junk folder.
  * Verify the email address entered.

* Expired reset link:

  * Request a new password reset email.

* Account locked:

  * Contact support for manual verification.

Security Notice:

Support agents will never ask for passwords.
Identity verification may be required before restoring access.
