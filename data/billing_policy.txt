Billing Policy

Billing Cycle:

* Customers are billed monthly.
* Subscription charges are automatically processed on the billing date.

Invoice Generation:

* Invoices are generated automatically.
* Customers can download invoices from the billing portal.

Duplicate Charges:

* Duplicate charges should be reported within 7 days.
* The billing team will investigate reported issues.

Payment Failure:

* Failed payments trigger automatic retry attempts.
* Customers will receive email notifications regarding failed transactions.

Billing Disputes:

* Billing disputes are reviewed by the finance team.
* Resolution is typically provided within 5 business days.
